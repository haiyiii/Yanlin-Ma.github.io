Panic Pusher

2017.12

Collaborative work with Chelsea Chen.

An installation that visualizes my email stress which is getting more and more intense as emails come in.

The concept comes from Tim Urban’s theory: there is an Instant Gratification Monkey exists in procrastinator’s brains. Every time when you are trying to finish your work, the monkey suddenly shows up, stops you and drags you into a place called the Dark Playground. However, the fun you have in the Dark Playground isn’t actually fun because it’s completely unearned and the within there is filled with guilt, anxiety, self-hatred, and dread. The only thing that can scare the Monkey away is called the Panic Monster who can be woken up when the deadline is close.
