Drifter

2017.12.08

Watch it Here

DRIFTER is a short animation film made for the 120 feet long video wall at the IAC Building in Chelsea, New York. A collaborative work with Siman Li. Original music by Lu Wang.

Inspired by some scenes of Ghost in the shell, the film is designed as a vision of one man's dream after a brocken relationship. What's your dream like? What can you see in your dreams? It also refers to all of us. We're all drifters, of the Earth and the universe. I'm hope we take that leap off the ground and permanently become drifters of the sky.
