Bean - rain transcoder over distance (in progress)

2017.01

Concept

Maintaining the quality and frequency of connection is challenging when people you care lives apart from one another. Technologies are cold but weather brings emotions. This project is about building something to necessarily support an emotional connection in an artistic way and allows you to feel the weather over distance..

“I left home six years ago and I really miss my hometown and lovely people there.”. “There is also a unique gratification with feeling the exact weather of the city you care.” Thus was born this project. I first though about making this project in an art installation way, but I wanna turn that into product level.


Audience

For people who miss a person or place over distance.


Method

Foley is the art of creating and putting sounds for picture. Not only the perfect composition but the props they use and the ideas they come up with really impress me a lot. In my project, I’ll be using some foley method to explore how to trancode and simulate the far-away rain sound and create meanings. So I decided to control some daily things to kind of trancode the rain data.
